# Nxturn — Marketing Document

---

## One-liner

> **Nxturn tells your patients exactly when to arrive — so your waiting room stays empty.**

---

## The Problem

Every day, sick patients leave home early, sit in crowded waiting rooms for 1–3 hours, and go home exhausted — before they've even seen the doctor.

For the clinic, it's just as painful:
- Patients crowd the reception asking "how long?"
- Receptionists lose time managing complaints instead of registrations
- No-shows and late arrivals disrupt the entire queue
- The doctor ends up rushing or sitting idle — never running smoothly

**This is not a logistics problem. It's a communication problem.**

Patients don't know when their turn is. So they show up early and wait.

---

## The Solution

**Nxturn** is an AI-powered queue management system built specifically for small clinics in India.

The moment a patient registers, they receive a WhatsApp message with a live link to track their turn. When it's time to head to the clinic, we message them again — with their exact estimated turn time.

**Patients wait at home. They arrive on time. Your waiting room stays calm.**

---

## How It Works

```
Patient registers at reception (30 seconds)
         ↓
WhatsApp sent: "You're #7. Track your turn: nxturn.app/status/7"
         ↓
AI monitors the queue in real time
         ↓
WhatsApp alert: "Your turn is at 6:20pm — head to clinic now"
         ↓
Patient arrives on time. Doctor sees them. Done.
```

No app download. No login. Works on any phone.

## Who It's For

**Ideal clinic profile:**
- 1 doctor, 1 receptionist
- 15–50 patients per day
- General practice / family medicine / pediatrics

**Not suitable for:**
- Hospitals with multiple departments

## Key Features

### For the Receptionist
- Register a patient in under 30 seconds — name, phone, symptoms
- One tap to move to the next patient
- Doctor delay button — notifies all patients automatically
- Priority queue for elderly, emergencies, pregnant patients
- Full patient list with live status

### For the Doctor
- Analytics dashboard — daily/weekly/monthly
- Avg consultation time per case type
- Most common symptoms
- No-show rate and patient feedback
- Export to CSV

### For the Patient
- WhatsApp notification on registration
- Live status page — no app needed
- Turn time prediction with 2-minute buffer
- Doctor delay alerts
- Self-cancel from their phone

### For the Clinic
- Token display screen for clinic TV (for patients without smartphones)
- End-of-day summary sent to doctor's WhatsApp
- Auto queue reset every midnight
- Historical data improves predictions over time

## The AI Advantage

Every time a patient is seen, the system learns:

| What it learns | How it helps |
|---|---|
| Avg time per case type | Predicts new vs follow-up accurately |
| Symptom-based durations | Chest pain takes longer than cold |
| Day-of-week patterns | Mondays are 20% slower |
| Morning vs evening pace | Post-lunch doctor is faster |
| Individual no-show history | Flags patients likely to skip |

**No retraining needed. Better data = better predictions automatically.**
