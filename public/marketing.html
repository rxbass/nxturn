The AI Engine

Gets smarter every single day

The more patients your clinic sees, the more accurate Nxturn's predictions become. No retraining. No configuration. Just better results over time.

Day-of-week patterns

Mondays are slower — it knows

The AI tracks your doctor's actual pace per weekday and adjusts predictions accordingly. Wednesday afternoons look different from Friday evenings.

Symptom complexity

Chest pain takes 18 mins. Cold takes 7.

By tracking actual consultation durations per symptom, the AI learns which cases are quick and which need extra time — before the patient walks in.

No-show detection

Flags patients likely to skip

Patients who have missed appointments before are flagged automatically. The AI accounts for this when calculating turn times for others.

Time-of-day pace

Post-lunch doctor is 20% faster

Morning, afternoon, and evening sessions have different rhythms. The AI tracks each separately and uses the right baseline for every prediction.

Priority routing

Elderly? Pregnant? Emergency? Front of queue.

Priority patients are flagged at registration and automatically moved to the front. The AI recalculates everyone else's time instantly.

Delay handling

Doctor 30 mins late? Everyone adjusted.

One tap logs the delay. The AI recalculates every waiting patient's turn time and sends them an updated WhatsApp — automatically.
